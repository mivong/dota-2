DOTA 2

Sniper

A long-range damage dealer with powerful abilities like Assassinate for finishing enemies.

Invoker

A complex hero with ten spells, ranging from crowd control to damage, based on the combination of Quas, Wex, and Exort.

Shadow Fiend

Shadow Fiend is a demon who gathers souls with every kill, increasing his damage. His abilities focus on controlling enemies and dealing massive burst damage.

Pudge

Pudge is a fearsome butcher known for his hooks and rot. He excels at singling out enemies and punishing them with his powerful disabling abilities.
